Register as an organisation which gets EU direct bid funding

This page is for UK grant recipients who:

  • are in receipt of funding from at least one of the funds below
  • have submitted a bid to one of the funds below
  • do not know if their bid will be awarded
  • have been awarded a grant, but do not presently have a grant agreement number

Do not register if:

  • you receive or have applied for funding from a different EU programme - check what you need to do instead
  • you're already in contact with a government department about what will happen to your funding after Brexit
  • your project will have finished before Brexit but the final payment will come after Brexit - contact helpdesk.grants.registration@cabinetoffice.gov.uk if you're told that you will not receive the final payment

Registering takes around 10 minutes.

Before you start

To register you need:

  • your Companies House or Charities Commission number
  • details from your grant agreement, if you have one

If you do not have a grant agreement, fill in as much of the information as possible and provide an explanation as to why in the 'Additional Comments' section.

If you are a recipient of multiple funds, complete separate templates for each fund (e.g. recipient of both Europe for Citizens and EaSI).

If you have questions about funding, contact the Cabinet Office Grants Management Function dealing with it.
