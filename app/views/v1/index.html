Register as an organisation which gets funding directly from the EU

You should register if you receive or have applied for funding from:

  • Civil Protection Mechanism
  • Competitiveness of Small and Medium-Sized Enterprises (COSME)
  • Connecting Europe Facility (CEF) telecoms
  • Creative Europe
  • Employment and Social Innovation (EaSI)
  • Erasmus+
  • Europe for Citizens
  • European Solidarity Corps
  • Health for Growth
  • Rights, Equality, and Citizenship Programme (RECP)

Do not register if:

  • you receive or have applied for funding from a different EU programme, such as Horizon 2020 - check what you need to do instead
  • you’re already in contact with a government department about what will happen to your funding after Brexit
  • your project will have finished before Brexit but the final payment will come after Brexit - contact the helpdesk if you’re told that you will not receive the final payment

Registering takes around 10 minutes.

Before you start

To register you need:

  • your Companies House or Charity Commission number, if you have one
  • details from your grant agreement, if you have one

If you do not have a grant agreement, fill in as much information as you can.

You’ll need to register again if you receive funding from more than one programme.
